🎯 WHAT YOU CAN NOW DO:

1. LOGIN/SIGNUP WITH PROVIDER:
   context.read<AuthProvider>().login(email, password);
   context.read<AuthProvider>().signup(name, email, password);

2. ACCESS AUTHENTICATED STATE:
   context.watch<AuthProvider>().isAuthenticated
   context.watch<AuthProvider>().user?.name

3. MANAGE PROGRAMS:
   context.read<ProgramProvider>().fetchPrograms();
   context.read<ProgramProvider>().selectProgram(program);
   context.read<ProgramProvider>().completeProgram(id);

4. CONTROL NAVIGATION:
   context.read<NavigationProvider>().onTapNav(index);

5. CONFIGURE APP SETTINGS:
   context.read<AppProvider>().toggleDarkMode();
